# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be a practical, respectful place to collaborate.

Examples of expected behavior:

- assume good intent
- give actionable, technically grounded feedback
- welcome newcomers and basic questions
- discuss tradeoffs without personal attacks
- keep criticism focused on the work, not the person

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or intimidation
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or sharing private information
- repeated hostile or bad-faith interactions
- publishing sensitive credentials or exploit details irresponsibly

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
